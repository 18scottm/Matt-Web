The Four Seasons of The United States

Spring

This season officially starts in March, then ends late June. Lots of rain in the season alone.

Summer

Starts in late June, then ends in late September / Early October. These months are extremely hot.

Autumn

Starts in September, ends in late November. These days are warm but not too hot, as time goes on it starts to get very cold.

Winter

The coldest time of the year. Starts in late November, ends in March.
